Shroomhearth utilizes a bespoke datapack for additional gameplay features.

Check out the datapack source or view the wiki for more information.

Shroomhearth runs on Fabric with some server-side mods:

  • Lithium
  • Blockbot
  • BlueMap
  • Ledger
  • Krypton
  • Simple Voice Chat
  • Carpet
  • HorseBuff
  • Shroomhearth's own Fabric mod

You can still connect to the server with a vanilla Minecraft client.
