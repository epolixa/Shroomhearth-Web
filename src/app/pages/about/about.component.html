The Story So Far...

The community originated from a similarly small Minecraft server named PurePhun that was started in 2013 by UburGeek. When PurePhun slowed down and became inactive, one of its players decided to create a server of their own to revive the PurePhun community.

8bitChap started the Minesmash server in the beginning of 2014 and drove it with an exceptional creative energy that delighted and engaged its players. 8bit would always be innovating with Minecraft both in-game and out, whether he was proposing new building projects, utilizing command blocks to invent ephemeral gameplay curios, or even filming cinematic stories with other players on the server. Many looked up to him as one of the best leaders a Minecraft server could have and imagined him going on to create the next Mindcrack or Hermitcraft. Unfortunately, 8bit passed away towards the end of 2014 and bequeathed the ownership of Minesmash onto a close family friend, Paraphernalien. Para was also a charismatic leader and did everything he could to nurture Minesmash's vivacity. Despite his efforts, he could not commit to the server in the same degree as 8bit and eventually more and more of his time became devoted to his family life. At the very least, Para ensured that Minesmash did not simply go dark with 8bit's passing and met a proper, delicate completion.

It wasn't long after Minesmash closed before a yearning to play Minecraft together again grew within its remaining community members. Finally epolixa, a friend of 8bitChap's and one of his employed moderators, decided to establish a new server with the purpose of keeping 8bit's creative spirit alive and bringing back the friends that were made along the way. And so, in December of 2014, the Minecraft server known as "Bityard" was born.

This server remains active to this day, after over 8 years of playtime, many updates to Minecraft, and several world resets.

Towards the end of 2021, Bityard was re-named to "Shroomhearth" in order to displace itself from a copyrighted cryptocurrency platform and foster its own unique identity as a Minecraft community.
